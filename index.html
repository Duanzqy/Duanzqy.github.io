About

Boyang Guo

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), where I am privileged to be advised by Prof. Juyong Zhang and Yudong Guo. Prior to my doctoral studies, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Science from USTC in 2025. My research interests lie at the intersection of Computer Vision and Graphics, with a specific focus on 3D Vision and Digital Humans. I am passionate about creating realistic, animatable digital representations of people..
